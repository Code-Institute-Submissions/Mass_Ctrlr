About the Band:

Hailing from the great northern powerhouse that is Manchester. Mass Ctrlr are regulars on the Manchester gig scene, receiving rave reviews for their energetic and crowd engaging performances. Jack, Chris, Josh and Tom make up Mass Ctrlr, an alternative rock band with nostalgia at its heart, Jack and Chris being the founding members.

Whilst nostalgic blues rock is the key foundation of their music, the band are never afraid to take influences from modern sounds such as hip hop. With jack occasionally bringing out his alter ego ‘burnout jack’ to spit some bars.

When Jack isn’t spitting bars he is captivating crowds with his powerful pipes and enigmatic stage presence. Lead guitarist Chris grew up in the great rock homeland of the midlands, which is obvious once you hear how the blue’s is entwined with his fantastic guitar style. Bass player josh is an experienced musician who has been in numerous bands over the years and adds an element of calm and much needed style to the band. Last but not least is the bands resident energiser bunny, Tom always brings the energy and was born to make to lots of noise and hit the skins.

Mass Ctrlr take pride in their live performances and have one mission when playing live, to entertain. The band has first and only release so far is the catchy and retro banger ‘Step Back’ which is a perfect example of what the band is all about. Head to our music page to check out 'Step Back' and to find out when we are playing next.

If you want to be entertained and enjoy feel good music, make sure you check them out!
