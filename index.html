About the Band:

Mass_Ctrlr are a 4 piece rock band from Manchester who started out in 2015 when a group of musical friends, who worked together at John Lewis, decided to start jamming together, writing songs which then turned into gigging. **Need to write a better blurb - get Jack to help**

Vocals: Lead Guitar: Bass Guitar: Drums:
Jack Armstrong Chris Reece Josh Stewart Tom Cliffe

Our Musical influences and inspirations are vaired which comes across in our music and performances...Imagine Foo fighters energetic drumming with Led Zeppelin vocals, mixed with bluesy guitar riffs

Mass Ctrlr are an up-and-coming Manchester band you do not want to miss live!
